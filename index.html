About Me

I am a QA engineer who builds reliable, maintainable quality gates. My approach blends clear communication, analytical thinking, and a strong product mindset so tests model real user behavior—not just the happy path. I care about fast, actionable feedback, readable reports, and zero surprises at release time.

My foundation is manual QA across web and mobile: precise test design in Zephyr, focused regression/smoke and exploratory sessions, and defect reports with full reproducible steps, impact, and evidence. I validate APIs with Postman and Swagger (auth tokens, response contracts, and data checks) and use SQL for end-to-end verification when it adds value.

In automation I work primarily with Playwright (JS/TS): modular Page Object patterns, stable semantic selectors, meaningful assertions, and a clean test-data strategy. I use API-driven setup where UI adds no value, reduce flakiness with deterministic waits, leverage trace/video for fast debugging, and integrate suites into CI to keep quality a first-class part of the delivery pipeline.
